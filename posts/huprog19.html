HUPROG'19 Hacettepe University Programming Competition

// under huprog




The greatest success of my life. HUPROG(Hacettepe University Programming Contest) is a competitive programming competition. It is one of the biggest competitive programming contests in Turkey. We host top programmer students around Turkey. I have been working for HUPROG for 3 years. I was the leader of this organization which becomes in 2019. Also, I led the question preparation team at the same time in 2019. We prepared questions.
What is this competition? It looks like an ACM ICPC. 470 teams joined in the competition but only 15 teams from all teams entitled to go to the final. The contest is performed on the "HackerRank".
