Reading/Watching/Listening

A few days ago I realized in 2023 I just 2 contributions on GitHub. Last year was the year I had the lowest number of contributions since I created my account in 2017. The empty cells didn't look very encouraging so I decided to change color of the contribution table. I want to see more green cells.

Since I do not have an open-source project that I can push a commit regularly to achieve my goal, I will share the articles I read, videos I watch, and podcasts I listen to every day to paint cells. This will motivate me to learn new things.
