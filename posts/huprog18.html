HUPROG'18 Hacettepe University Programming Competition

// under huprog




HUPROG(Hacettepe University Programming Contest) is a competitive programming competition. We are the organizers of first the first and only programming contest of our university and we reached out to top programmer students, encouraged them in competitive programming for 2 years. I was a member of the preparing questions team of HUPROG’17-’18.
What is this competition? It is like an ACM ICPC. More than 100 teams joined the competition but only 15 teams from all teams entitled to go to the offline final. The contest is performed on the "HackerRank".
